Learning and Interactive Explanation
Molarity is defined as:
$$\text{Molarity} = {\text{Number of Moles} \over \text{Volume in Leaters}}$$
To calculate the number of moles we can multiply by the volume in leters
$$\text{Moles} = \text{Molarity} \times \text{Volume in Leaters} = {\text{Number of Moles} \over \cancel{\text{Volume in Leaters}}} \times {\cancel{\text{Volume in Leaters}} \over 1} $$
To calculate the relationship between two solutions we can use the formula
$$ M_{1}V_{1} = M_{2}V_{2} $$
